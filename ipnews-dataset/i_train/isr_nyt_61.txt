israel holds 463 arabs accused in assaults israeli security forces have arrested 463 members of the popular front for the liberation of palestine over the last few weeks the israeli army said today the sweep which israel television described as the largest ever of front members took place throughout the israeli occupied west bank gaza strip and arab east jerusalem two youths sought in the stabbing death of a jewish theology student in jerusalem s old city on feb 28 were among those detained the army said many of those arrested were suspected of involvement in attacks involving firearms there has recently been a rise in use of firearms in the palestinian revolt against israeli occupation that had mainly employed stones and firebombs against armed israeli soldiers earlier police said a 67 year old jew was stabbed to death apparently in revenge for the killing by the israeli police of 17 arabs at jerusalem s temple mount last year
