november 8 1986 saturday late city final edition israeli police summon 4 who met with p l o the police have summoned for questioning 4 of the 27 israelis who returned today from a meeting with palestine liberation organization representatives in rumania the israeli radio reported the four israelis were handed the summons orders when they landed in ben gurion international airport today it was the first time israelis had openly met with p l o members since a law banning meetings with terrorist organizations was passed in august latif dori a delegation organizers who received a summons said the trip had been a protest against the law the offense carries a penalty of up to three years in jail the meeting broken off early reportedly because of arab pressure or terrorist threats against the participants
